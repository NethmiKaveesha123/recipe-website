Ingredients

  • Spaghetti or other pasta
  • Pancetta or bacon, diced
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Eggs
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta with garlic until crisp.
  3. Beat eggs and Parmesan together in a bowl, seasoning with pepper.
  4. Drain pasta and mix with the pancetta in the pan.
  5. Remove from heat, add egg mixture, and stir quickly until creamy.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan and pepper.
