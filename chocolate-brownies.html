Ingredients

  • Unsweetened chocolate
  • Butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Chopped nuts or chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Melt chocolate and butter together.
  2. Stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Add flour and salt, mix until just combined.
  4. Fold in nuts or chocolate chips if using.
  5. Pour batter into a prepared baking pan and bake until set.
  6. Cool in the pan, then cut into squares and serve.
